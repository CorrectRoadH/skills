# Startup Mirror — Adversarial Startup Idea Stress Test (Loot Drop Methodology)

An AI agent skill that runs a ruthless, structured pre-mortem on a startup idea using the [Loot Drop](https://www.loot-drop.io) framework — 7 failure antipatterns distilled from 1,628+ dead-company autopsies. The goal is not validation. It's finding the antipattern that will kill the company before the founder spends a year discovering it themselves.

Think: YC-office-hours-meets-post-mortem. An adversarial second opinion, not a cheerleader.

## What it does

Runs a 5-phase review of your idea:

1. **Gather** — product, customer, monetization, stage, runway
2. **The Mirror** — score 0/1/2 against each of the 7 antipatterns:
   - 🔮 **The Hallucination** — building for a customer that doesn't exist
   - 🔥 **The Bonfire** — spending as if revenue is a formality
   - ⚔️ **The Civil War** — team/founder conflict destroys the company
   - 🏴 **The Crushed** — out-executed, out-spent, out-gunned (**54% of all deaths**)
   - 🍋 **The Lemon** — product cannot deliver the promise
   - 📉 **The Math Problem** — every sale loses money at scale
   - ⚖️ **The Outlaw** — regulation is the immovable object
3. **The Cascade** — trace how the top antipatterns chain (Hallucination → Bonfire → Crushed is typical)
4. **Autopsies** — 2–3 real dead-startup cases in your sector with the same dominant antipattern
5. **The Rebuild Plan** — what to build, market analysis, build steps, tech stack, revenue model — each retiring a specific risk

Output ends with a **Risk Retirement Ledger** — every antipattern mapped to the plan step that retires it. Unretired risks are called out.
